Documenting your surroundings is a delicate art. In today's era of over-documentation, it can often feel like one is 'doing too much'. But in avoiding this pitfall, many people fall into the reverse trap of not doing enough and limiting their expression because of fear of judgement. Expressing what you find beautiful and important to others is one of the deepest parts of the human experience. Of course, expressing your human experience is different for each individual, and your selfies probably don't compare to Michaelango's expressions of his human experience. But regardless, if your expression of self takes form in iphone snaps or even your latest selfie, by all means, express it.
